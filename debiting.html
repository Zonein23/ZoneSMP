About Debit Cards & ZN's

Your debit card stores rupees, but in ZoneSMP, every rupee you have is converted to Zoneps (ZN's) at a rate of 2× the rupee amount.
For example, if you have ₹1,000, you get 2,000 ZN's!
Example: 100 rupees × 2 = 200 ZN's
